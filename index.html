Righteous Harmony

Righteous Harmony is a heartfelt, high-energy tribute to the legendary Righteous Brothers - the originators of blue-eyed soul. This concert experience celebrates their soaring harmonies, emotional storytelling, and iconic chart-toppers that defined a generation. Audiences are taken on a powerful musical journey, not only through the Righteous Brothers' own groundbreaking hits, but also through the artists who inspired them - like Sam Cooke - and the countless contemporary performers they've influenced, from Ed Sheeran to Adele This dynamic tribute show crosses genres and generations: soul, rock, pop, and everything in between - a timeless celebration of love, loss, longing, and harmony.
